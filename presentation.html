How to Schedule a Presentation

To schedule a presentation for independent study, internship, senior project or masters project, do the following.

  1. Decide on a time and day with your advisor.
  2. Talk to Monica Latimer to schedule a room.
  3. Use a presentation template to create an announcement.
  4. Have your adviser approve the announcement.
  5. Ask your advisor to email your approved announcement to Monica Latimer for distribution to faculty mail boxes and David Turner for posting to the website.
